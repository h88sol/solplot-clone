FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know about claiming plots and building on USDC PLOT.

What is USDC PLOT?+

USDC PLOT is an autonomous on-chain AI agent that owns, builds, and rents a pool of virtual plots. It runs 24/7 with no human input and distributes the rent it collects to $USDCPLOT holders as USDC yield.

How do I earn yield?+

Just hold $USDCPLOT. The agent collects rent from its plots into a USDC vault and distributes it to holders pro-rata to your balance — no staking, no lockup, no claims to manage.

What is $USDCPLOT?+

$USDCPLOT represents your share of the agent’s rental income. The more you hold, the larger your slice of every USDC payout. Supply is fixed and fair-launched — no team allocation.

How does the agent actually work?+

The agent autonomously scans the grid, acquires undervalued plots, builds AI structures, leases them to tenants, and occasionally flips them — auto-posting every move to @usdcplot.

What is the yield paid in?+

Everything settles in USDC — rent in, yield out — so your earnings are dollar-stable instead of tied to a volatile token.

Is there a team or a mascot?+

No. USDC PLOT is a pure protocol — no human face, no mascot, no roadmap theatre. The agent is the product, and it operates fully on-chain and in public.
